MONKEY TRAIL

The Monkey Trail start is reached after cycling the first 3.7km/2.29 miles of Follow the Dog Trail. The trail is managed in partnership with Chase Trails.

  • The Monkey Trail was built and designed in partnership with Chase Trails and features technical rock gardens, boardwalks, jumps and drops.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

The Monkey Trail is a mix of fast and flowing machine built trail and tight, twisty and technical handbuilt trails created by the Chase Trails volunteers
